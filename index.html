How I work

Engineering discipline a project manager can rely on

Three things I focus on when I take a board from idea to hardware.

01

Full design cycle, one owner

I handle schematic capture, component selection, layout, and routing myself in Altium Designer — one point of accountability from concept to a manufacturable board.

02

Built with test in mind

DFM, ESD protection, and how a board will actually be probed and validated are part of the layout decisions, not something figured out after it's built.

03

Clear documentation

ERC-clean schematics, organized layer stacks, and notes that a test or production team can follow without needing to ask me first.
